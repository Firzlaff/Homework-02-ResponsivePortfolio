About Me

I was born in St. Helena Hospital, lake County California, April 30th 1987, I grew up in Cobb California until moving to Sandy Utah when i was in Kindergarten. I love the outdoors and living on the Wasatch Mountain range. Most my lifSe I have lived on the mountain skiing, I truly believe when they say that Utah has the best snow on earth that it is 100% the truth. Skiing through our powder with no consequence drops is a rush like none other, explaining to people the feeling of drowning in waist high snow flowing over your whole body engulfing you as begin making your first turns decending the mountain, that feeling when you float to the top and break through the beautiful air and sun, riding the wave of snow down incredible steeps feeling like your floating through a cloud of goose down feathers of cold. Not only did I ski but Mountain Biking, hiking, and trail running also occupy much of my summers. Utah feels special with also having Moab and the Red Rocks just south of us, offering another beautiful landscape for outdoor fun. Lately I do not get out as much as I am spending more time expanding my knowledge of Tech/PC/Coding world. I have always been facinating by programing and the creating that can be done with computers. When I do find myself outside its generally hiking going on walks playing pokemonGo or doing a Spartan Race.

At a young age I realized I had a passion for all electronics and tech, this with gaming and the outdoors have been all my passions and hobbies for the past 25 years.
